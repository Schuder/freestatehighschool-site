Seniors Wishing to Attend KU or KSU

Deadline for Seniors wishing to attend KU and KSU: NOW start gathering materials needed to apply for admission and scholarships. Request a copy of your transcript to be sent electronically through parchment.com several days before the November 1, 2013 deadline. All materials must be submitted by the November 1, 2013 deadline to be considered for scholarships and the University Honors Program. You can take or retake the ACT/SAT and submit the score to KU. KU's codes are 1470 for the ACT and 6871 for the SAT. Your last opportunity to take either test and be considered for KU scholarships is in December.

FAFSA 2013-14 - January 1, 2014

FAFSA 2013-14 - January 1, 2014 The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA (http://fafsa.gov) becomes available online. You must file this form to be considered for federal loans, grants, and Work-Study and some state and university aid. March 1, 2014 is the priority deadline to file the FAFSA to be eligible for funds. After March 1 awards may be smaller.

TRANSCRIPTS HAVE GONE GREEN!

2002 Graduates to present will place request for transcripts online.
Here's how the secure transcript delivery works:
• Go to Docufide by Parchment Click on "Sign up" in the lower right hand corner of the screen.
• Create an account
• Select Lawrence Free State High School as your high school.
• Select your transcript destination(s)
• Confirm your selections
• Read and accept the user agreement

After you have filled out all of the required information, a confirmation email will be sent to your email account. You must click on the link to activate your account. Docufide by Parchment will deliver your official school verified and approved transcript to the colleges or destinations you have chosen. For other organizations, such as scholarship providers, click the link under "Select Other Destinations" or "NCAA" and enter the information requested. You will receive an email confirmation when each transcript is delivered, and for colleges that accept transcripts electronically, when they receive it as well.

***If you have any problems during the registration or ordering process please go to www.docufide.com click on "contact us" select "students/parents" and fill out the form with your name, school name, and details of the issue you are having.

Skyward Parent Access

If you have not utilized the system, please email Teresa Arevalo, Registrar, at tarevalo@usd497.org to sign up. Your account must be confirmed and activated prior to its availability.
